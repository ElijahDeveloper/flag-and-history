About Flag Cards

Welcome to Flag Cards — your interactive guide to learning about countries’ flags and histories. Click any flag on the home page to dive into a concise summary of its nation’s background. This project was built as a fun way to blend design, JavaScript DOM manipulation, and historical trivia.

Features:

  • Responsive card layout
  • Modal popups for in‑depth history
  • Mobile‑friendly navigation
